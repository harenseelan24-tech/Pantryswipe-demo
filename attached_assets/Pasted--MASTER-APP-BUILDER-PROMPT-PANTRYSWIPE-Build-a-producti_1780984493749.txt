# MASTER APP-BUILDER PROMPT — PANTRYSWIPE

Build a production-ready mobile application called **PantrySwipe** for iOS, Android, and Web.

This is not simply a recipe application.

PantrySwipe is an AI-powered cooking ecosystem that combines recipe discovery, pantry management, meal planning, grocery tracking, event planning, nutrition tracking, social networking, and food waste reduction into a single platform.

The experience should feel like a combination of Tinder, TikTok, Instagram, MyFitnessPal, Pinterest, and a personal AI chef.

The primary goal is to answer one question:

"What can I cook with what I already have?"
