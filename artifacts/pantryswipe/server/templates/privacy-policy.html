Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

PantrySwipe is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use the PantrySwipe mobile application.

By using PantrySwipe, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We will never sell your personal data to third parties.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use PantrySwipe:

  • Name and email address (for account creation)
  • Pantry inventory and ingredient lists
  • Barcode scan history
  • Saved and favourite recipes
  • Notification preferences
  • Device information (model, OS version, app version)
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Anonymous usage analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Create and manage your PantrySwipe account
  • Synchronise your pantry across devices
  • Generate personalised AI recipe suggestions
  • Improve barcode recognition accuracy
  • Send expiry reminders and notifications
  • Improve overall app performance and reliability
  • Provide customer support when requested

4. Third-Party Services

PantrySwipe may integrate with the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy:

  • Supabase — secure database and authentication
  • Open Food Facts — open-source food product database
  • UPCitemDB — barcode lookup database
  • OpenAI (or other AI providers) — recipe generation
  • Google Firebase — analytics and crash reporting (if enabled)
  • Apple Push Notification Service — expiry reminders (iOS)
  • Google Play Services — notifications and services (Android)

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party providers. We are not responsible for their data practices.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your pantry information and personal data are securely stored using encrypted databases. We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest to protect your information from unauthorised access.

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • View all personal information we hold about you
  • Update or correct your account information
  • Delete individual pantry items at any time
  • Delete your PantrySwipe account entirely
  • Request removal of all stored personal data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@pantryswipe.app. We will respond to all requests within 30 days.

7. Cookies & Analytics

PantrySwipe may collect anonymous usage statistics to help us understand how the app is used and how to improve it. This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you.

You can opt out of analytics collection at any time in Settings → Privacy → Analytics.

8. Children's Privacy

PantrySwipe is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will notify you through the app and update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

Continued use of PantrySwipe after changes are made constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

support@pantryswipe.app
